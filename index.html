About Star Nails Salon

Our Star Nails Salon is dedicated to bringing top-of-the-line products mixed with expert techniques to the Star Nails Salon industry. Offering many services such as Manicure, Pedicure, and Artificial Nails allows us to be a one-stop destination for those looking for a complete rejuvenating experience. The friendly staff creates an atmosphere of urban relaxation. We are always trying to be innovative with design and trend, always up-to-date with what the industry has to offer.
